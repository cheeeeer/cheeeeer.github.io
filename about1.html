About Us

A Frog and a Cat Productions was created in early 2017 when we decided we wanted an identity to share our content under. This led to the formation of A Frog and a Cat Productions, and ever since then all of our unfinished projects have had the label slapped on them. Anything of somewhat passable quality will probably be released on here.

The origin of the name A Frog and a Cat Productions is completely forgotten. Sorry.
