EGG RPG

EGG RPG is a post-apocalyptic sci-fi Role-Playing Game that focuses on light, easy to pick up rules and a very open approach to mechanics, allowing players to quickly become immersed in the lore and begin playing.

Features

  • Easy to pick up ruleset with quick character creation
  • A unique, post-apocalyptic sci-fi setting
  • Deadly, gory combat full of dismemberment and explosions
  • Super cool bionic limbs and eyes
